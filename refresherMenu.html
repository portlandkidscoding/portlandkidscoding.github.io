Refresher Lessons

We decided to create and post these refresher lessons to help out with students that prefer to learn by seeing the concept visually at their workstation after the in-class demonstration, or for students that remember 90% of what they're trying to do but need a refresher on a piece of syntax.

Increasing the number of accessible resources out there is rarely a bad idea.

Currently this page is still a work in progress, I've just decided to push what I had. More lessons will be added!
